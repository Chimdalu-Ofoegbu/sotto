THE INSIGHT

Privacy as a protocol property — not a promise.

Canton enforces sub-transaction privacy. A rival never sees your bid because the ledger never reveals it to them — no auctioneer to trust, no ZK circuit to audit.

THE MECHANISM

Sealed bids in, one winner out, atomic settlement.

Invited counterparties submit sealed bids. The seller clears to the best one and asset-for-cash settles in a single transaction. Losers get escrow back and never learn the price.
